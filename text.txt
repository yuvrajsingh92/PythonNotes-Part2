Hello Ayush Singh

Deep within the Whispering Woods, an ancient oak tree guarded a hidden doorway. For centuries, travelers searched for the key, unaware that the forest itself was alive. A young mapmaker named Leo stepped onto the mossy path, carrying nothing but a brass compass and a blank journal. The trees began to murmur, their leaves rustling a cryptic warning into the wind. Suddenly, a silver fox appeared from the shadows, locking its bright eyes with Leo's. It gestured toward the roots of the giant oak, where a single golden key gleamed beneath the dirt. Leo smiled, realizing his journey was only just beginning.
